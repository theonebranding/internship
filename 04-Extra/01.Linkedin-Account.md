

# **LinkedIn Account Setup Guide for Interns**

Follow the instructions below to create your LinkedIn profile, complete all required details, add your internship, and make your first post.

---

## **1. Create Your LinkedIn Account**

### Steps:

1. Go to **[https://www.linkedin.com/](https://www.linkedin.com/)**
2. Click **Join now** or **SignUp with Google**.
3. Enter your **email address** and **password**.
4. Add your **full name** exactly as you use professionally.
5. Verify your **email** using the code sent to your inbox.
6. Add your **country** and **city**.
7. Choose **I’m a student** or **I’m working**, whichever is correct.
8. Continue following the setup prompts until your account is ready.

---

## **2. Add a Profile Photo and Banner**

### Profile Photo:

1. Open LinkedIn and click **Me → View Profile**.
2. Click the **camera icon** on the profile photo.
3. Upload a **professional-looking image** (clear face, simple background).

### Banner (Background Image):

1. On your profile, click the **camera icon** on the banner area.
2. Add a simple professional banner (solid color, gradient, or tech-related graphic).

This makes your profile look polished and complete.

---

## **3. Add Your Education Information**

### Steps:

1. Go to your profile → scroll to the **Education** section.
2. Click **Add education**.
3. Fill in:

   * **School/College name**
   * **Degree or course**
   * **Start date – End date**
   * **Description** (optional)
4. Click **Save**.

Your education section helps recruiters understand your background.

---

## **5. Add Your Internship to the Experience Section**

You must add your ongoing internship to your LinkedIn profile.

### Steps:

1. Go to your profile page.

2. Scroll to **Experience**.

3. Click **Add experience**.

4. Fill the details:

   * **Title:** Frontend Intern
   * **Employment type:** Internship
   * **Company:** The One Branding
     (select the correct company with the logo)
   * **Location:** (Add your city)
   * **Start date:** **4th November 2025**
   * **End date:** Select **Present**
   * **Description:**
     You can mention you are learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub, and working on real projects.

5. Click **Save**.

---

## **6. Share a Post Announcing Your New Position**

After adding the internship, share a short post to show that you’ve started a new role.

### Steps:

1. Go to your LinkedIn home page.
2. Click **Start a post**.
3. Write something like:

*“Excited to start my journey as a **Frontend Intern** at **The One Branding**. Looking forward to learning, growing, and contributing to meaningful projects!”*

4. Add:

   * A **photo** (optional)
   * Tag **The One Branding** (optional)
5. Click **Post**.

Posting about your new role helps you build visibility and start networking.
