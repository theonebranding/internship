
**Interns, Be ready for your video testimonial!
Please cover the points below in 1–2 minutes:**

1. Start by introducing yourself: *What’s your name, your college, your branch/stream, and your current semester or year?*

2. Say this line clearly:
   **“I am currently doing my internship at The One Branding as a Frontend Developer Intern.”**

3. Share what you’ve learned during the internship — for example:
   *HTML, CSS, JavaScript, VS Code, Git, GitHub.*

4. Talk about the project you built:
   *What’s the project title? What is it about? What did you create or learn while making it?*

5. Describe your overall experience during the internship.

6. Talk a bit about the company environment —
   *How did it feel working at The One Branding?*

7. Share a few words about your mentor, **Yasin Vahora** —
   *How did he guide or support you during the internship?*

8. End by expressing your gratitude to **The One Branding** for giving you the internship opportunity.
